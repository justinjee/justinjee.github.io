I don't totally get this whole science thing. On the one hand, it's supposed to help us stay honest. On the other hand, a lot of it is moving in a direction where the most popular findings are the ones that have the most expensive equipment and the most collaborators. Not that there's anything wrong with sequencing experiments or high-energy particle physics experiments, and I definitely have nothing against collaborators! I just think a little individuality is being lost along the way. And if running Southern Blots can't be intimately intertwined into your own individuality, are they really worth doing?

Despite this, I do research, because I think it is worth it: Me@NYU
